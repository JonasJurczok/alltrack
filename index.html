Welcome to Alltrack.

AllTrack is an Android app that solves one problem that bugged me for a long time: general tracking of things. There are hundreds of apps out there that do specific tracking for you: weight, running time, money spending etc. But what about generic things? Number of people you talked to during a day? Number of people who attend your talks?

Either you install (and find!) a specific solution to this problem or you use AllTrack. With this application you can configure multiple datasets and then add values to them.
